Proton Beam Precision

We replace wasteful diamond saws with a high-current proton particle accelerator. Our proprietary system embeds hydrogen ions deep into bulk GaN or SiC crystals.

This allows controlled exfoliation of ultra-thin active layers without mechanical grinding, drastically reducing material usage.
